The beauty of portugal in lisbon

Portugal is a breathtakingly beautiful country, blending dramatic Atlantic coastlines, golden Algarve beaches, and historic, charming cities like Lisbon and Porto. It boasts a rich, romantic atmosphere with tiled palaces, 150+ castles, and scenic, terraced vineyards in the Douro Valley, often cited as a top European destination for both natural beauty and culture.
